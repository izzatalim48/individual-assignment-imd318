My Hobby

One of my favorite hobbies is playing games, especially those about Spider-Man. I've always loved immersing myself in his world, swinging through New York City, and battling iconic villains like Green Goblin and Doctor Octopus. It’s a thrilling experience that allows me to feel like a hero. Beyond gaming, my passion for Spider-Man extends to attending themed events and collecting comics.

Joining Events

I’ve always had a deep love for Spider-Man, so when the opportunity arose, I couldn’t resist the chance to bring my favorite hero to life by doing a Spider-Man cosplay. It was an amazing experience, and what made it even better was that it took place during a Marvel event at Toy R Us. The atmosphere was electric, filled with excitement as fans of all ages gathered to celebrate everything Marvel. As I suited up in my Spider-Man costume, I felt an incredible sense of pride. It was like stepping into the shoes of a superhero, embodying the spirit of Spider-Man and getting to interact with other Marvel fans who shared the same passion.
